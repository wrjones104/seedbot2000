--------------------------------------------
**Practice Arguments**
--------------------------------------------
Practice ROM allows you to fight Final Kefka using the airship controls, and fight any boss by interacting with Gehstahl.
Interact with Leo to recruit characters onto your team as you will start with 4 random characters. Then use airship guy to swap party members and equip everyone prior to the final battle.
The practice ROM has several different modes & options which are outlined here:
